36.15.1.1 Arabic Letter Category

Heh Doachashmee Group

The Arabic letter Heh Doachashmee does not generally have the same appearance as the letter Heh, but some fonts nonetheless assign them the same glyph. This causes some PDF creators to confuse the two characters. This warning group permits treating that as a warning. Such warnings should always be checked visually.
