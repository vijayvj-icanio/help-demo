18.8 Dislocated word and reference boxes

 

When projects referring to non-PDF documents (e.g. RTF, TXT) are opened on a different computer, it is possible that fonts used to render the documents initially are not present. TVT will choose replacement fonts to render the text, but the character properties will not necessarily be kept. Text rendering will be different causing differences in line and page breaks.

As word boxes (the rectangle highlighting of each word recognized by TVT before the comparison) are calculated only at the start of the project, they stay at the same place even when the project is loaded on a different computer.
